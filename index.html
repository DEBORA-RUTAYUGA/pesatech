About Us

PesaTech
Accelerator

Pesatech Accelerator is a fintech accelerator program designed to support post-MVP and growth-stage fintech startups in Tanzania. The accelerator aims to build fintech's capacity to raise investment through an Investor-Readiness Program (IRP) and act as a marketplace between startups, strategic partners, and investors.

Process

The accelerator will take the startups through the following processes

Opening and
Launching

Opening the call for application and launching the accelerator with strategic partners in the startup ecosystem.

Virtual Assessment (InvestoRedo)

Running of virtual assessment of the applications received via the InvestoRedo platform.

Physical Assessment (Diagnosis)

Running physical assessment and diagnosis of the post revenue startups for investor readiness support.

Bootcamp (Post MVP Startups)

Two weeks technical and business bootcamp for registered post MVP startups to improve quality of the products and support on go-to-market strategy.

One-On-One Session (Post Revenue Startups)

Series of experts and mentors meeting for registered post revenue startups to help with go-to-market strategy, compliance issues and fundraising.

Demo Day & Post Acceleration Support

Deal sessions and marketplace to support startups access strategic partnerships and potential investors.
